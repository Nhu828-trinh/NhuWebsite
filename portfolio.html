Carnival Fish Catching Game

In this project we made a Carnival Fish Catching Game where we a have a pool of fish and a net, the objective is to catch the fish by clicking on it in order to score a point, and you have to catch as much fishes as possible under 10 seconds, after 10 seconds, the turtle will stop and you will not be able to catch anymore fish.
